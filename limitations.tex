Our results are validated on two Qwen2.5-family models (7B and 1.5B); generalization to other architecture families (e.g., LLaMA, Mistral) and larger scales remains open.
We evaluate on three benchmarks (GSM8K, MATH-500, MBPP); logical, commonsense, and multi-modal reasoning are not covered.
\rasp{} $\alpha$ variants and Random use single GRPO seeds, so fine-grained ranking among these should be interpreted with caution.
